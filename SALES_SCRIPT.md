## SCRIPT 1: COLD CALL (2 minutes)

### Opening (10 seconds)

> "Hi [Name], this is Eddy from InvoiceShield. I know you're in the middle of a job so I'll be quick, 60 seconds. Fair?"

*[Wait for yes]*

### The Hook (15 seconds)

> "I'm calling contractors in [City] about a problem I keep hearing: late payments. 91% of Canadian contractors deal with it. Are you one of them?"

*[Let them talk. They WILL talk. This is their biggest pain.]*

### Listen & Validate (30 seconds)

Whatever they say, validate it:
- "60 days? That's brutal."
- "Yeah, I hear that a lot. One guy told me he's owed $40K right now."
- "So you're basically financing their project with your own money."

### The Pitch (30 seconds)

> "That's exactly why I built InvoiceShield. It's an AI tool that sends professional follow-up emails and texts on your behalf. The first reminder is friendly, a polite nudge. Then it automatically escalates. Firm, then final notice. 68% of late invoices get paid after just the first nudge."

### The Closer (15 seconds)

> "Here's the best part: you only pay a small percentage when you actually get paid. No big monthly fee, no collection agency taking 25 cents of every dollar. On a $10K invoice, a collection agency takes $2,500. We take about $300. You keep $9,700."

### The Ask (10 seconds)

> "Can I send you a 2-minute demo link? Or I can walk you through it right now on your phone. Takes less time than chasing one invoice."

### If they say yes:

> "Great. What's the best email? I'll send the link right now. It's invoiceshield.netlify.app. You can sign up for early access and I'll personally onboard you this week."

### If they say "send me info":

> "Absolutely. I'll email you a one-pager right now. Quick question before I go: roughly how many invoices are you waiting on right now?"

*[This keeps the conversation going and qualifies them]*

### If they say "I'm busy":

> "Totally get it. When's a better time? I'll call you back in 2 minutes flat. This could save you thousands."

## QUALIFYING QUESTIONS (Ask These on Every Call)

1. **"How many invoices are you waiting on right now?"** (Quantifies the pain)
2. **"Roughly how much are you owed in total?"** (Sizes the opportunity)
3. **"What's the longest you've waited for payment?"** (Emotional trigger)
4. **"What do you do when someone doesn't pay?"** (Identifies current solution)
5. **"Do you use any invoicing software?"** (Determines integration path)
6. **"Have you ever used a collection agency?"** (Price anchor: we're 10x cheaper)
